Buttery Flaky Pie Crust Recipe

Description

This pie crust recipe made with butter produces a consistent dough that bakes into a crisp and flaky pie crust every time. It's perfect for beginner bakers and seasoned professionals!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/4 Tsp Salt
  • 1/2 Cup Butter, diced and then chilled
  • 1/4 Cup ice cold water, or as needed

Steps

  1. Combine the flour and salt.
  2. Cut in the cold butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Add the cold water a tablespoon at a time.
  4. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  5. Roll the dough into a circle, then press it into a pie plate. Trim the excess and flute the edges.
